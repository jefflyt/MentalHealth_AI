## üí∞ Financial Assistance & Insurance

### Medisave Coverage
- **Mental Health Conditions Covered**: Schizophrenia, bipolar disorder, major depression, anxiety disorders
- **Coverage**: Up to $300 per year for outpatient psychiatric treatment
- **Requirements**: Specialist referral required
- **Eligible Treatments**: Psychiatric consultations, approved medications

### Community Health Assist Scheme (CHAS)
- **Eligibility**: Singapore citizens and PRs with household income ‚â§ $1,800/month
- **Subsidies**: Up to 75% off specialist consultations
- **Coverage**: IMH outpatient services, selected private clinics
- **Application**: Through any polyclinic or online at CHAS website

### Medifund
- **Purpose**: Safety net for those who cannot afford medical bills
- **Coverage**: Up to 100% subsidy for eligible patients
- **Eligibility**: Means-tested, for Singapore citizens
- **Application**: Through hospital medical social workers

### Insurance Coverage
- **Private Insurance**: Most plans cover psychiatric treatments with prior approval
- **Integrated Shield Plans**: Cover inpatient psychiatric care
- **Employee Benefits**: Many employers provide EAP (Employee Assistance Programs)

## üí° Tips for Choosing Services

### Public vs Private
- **Public**: More affordable, longer wait times, require referrals
- **Private**: Faster access, higher costs, direct appointment

### What to Look For
- **Language preferences**: Ensure therapist speaks your preferred language
- **Specialization**: Match therapist expertise to your specific needs
- **Location**: Consider travel time and accessibility
- **Cost**: Factor in insurance coverage and subsidies
- **Availability**: Check waiting times and appointment flexibility

### Questions to Ask
- What is your experience with my specific concern?
- What therapy approaches do you use?
- How long are sessions and how often?
- What are the costs and payment options?
- Do you accept insurance or Medisave?
