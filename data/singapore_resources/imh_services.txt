Institute of Mental Health (IMH) Services for Singapore Youth

The Institute of Mental Health (IMH) is Singapore's leading specialist psychiatric hospital and the national tertiary referral center for mental health. IMH provides comprehensive mental health services for individuals of all ages, including specialized programs for youth.

IMH Emergency Department (24/7)
Phone: 6389-2222
Address: 10 Buangkok View, Singapore 539747
Services: 
- Emergency psychiatric care and crisis intervention
- Immediate assessment for mental health emergencies
- Acute psychiatric treatment
- Safety evaluations
- Crisis stabilization
Operating Hours: 24 hours, 7 days a week
Cost: Emergency consultation fees apply, subsidies available based on means testing

IMH Outpatient Clinic
Phone: 6389-2000 (General Enquiries)
Phone: 6389-2200 (Appointment Scheduling)
Operating Hours: 
- Monday to Friday: 8:00 AM - 5:30 PM
- Saturday: 8:00 AM - 1:00 PM
- Closed on Sundays and public holidays

Services Offered:
- Psychiatric consultation and assessment
- Individual and group therapy sessions
- Medication management and monitoring
- Psychological testing and evaluation
- Family therapy and counseling
- Specialized youth mental health programs

Cost Structure:
- Subsidized rates for Singapore citizens and permanent residents
- Means testing available for additional subsidies
- Private rates for non-residents
- Medisave and insurance claims accepted
- Financial assistance programs available for those in need

Specialized Youth Programs:
1. Child and Adolescent Psychiatry Service
   - Comprehensive assessment and treatment for youth under 18
   - Family-centered approach to care
   - School liaison services

2. Early Psychosis Intervention Programme (EPIP)
   - Specialized care for first-episode psychosis
   - Age range: 16-40 years
   - Comprehensive treatment and support

3. Youth Mental Wellness Service
   - Preventive and early intervention programs
   - Mental health education and awareness
   - Community outreach initiatives

How to Access IMH Services:
1. Emergency Situations: Walk-in to IMH Emergency Department
2. Non-Emergency: 
   - Referral from family doctor/polyclinic
   - Direct appointment scheduling (call 6389-2200)
   - Online appointment booking through SingHealth website

3. CHAT Referral: Can be referred from CHAT centers for specialized care

What to Expect:
- Initial assessment appointment (typically 1-2 hours)
- Comprehensive evaluation of mental health status
- Development of personalized treatment plan
- Regular follow-up appointments as needed
- Coordination with other healthcare providers

Important Notes:
- Bring NRIC/identification and relevant medical records
- Arrive 30 minutes early for appointments
- Inform staff of any current medications
- Family members encouraged to participate in treatment (with patient consent)
- Interpreter services available if needed

IMH also provides:
- Inpatient psychiatric services
- Day rehabilitation programs
- Community mental health services
- Research and training programs
- Mental health education resources

For non-emergency inquiries or to learn more about specific services, visit the IMH website or call the general enquiry line at 6389-2000.
